Our Vision & Mission

At Hayai Ramen, our mission is to serve high-quality Japanese ramen with speed, precision, and passion. We combine fresh ingredients, efficient service, and warm hospitality to deliver a flavorful experience in every bowl—because great ramen shouldn’t make you wait.

Original Ramen

Our Original Ramen is a timeless bowl of comfort, made with rich, slow-cooked broth and springy handmade noodles.

Shoyu Ramen

Balanced, savory broth with tender noodles and rich toppings.

Spicy Tonkotsu

A bold kick in every slurp—perfect for spice lovers.

Vegetarian Ramen

Rich umami flavor made 100% plant-based and nourishing.

Our growth in numbers

Since opening its first store in 2019, Hayai Ramen has grown rapidly—expanding to five cities, serving thousands of customers monthly, and earning a 96% satisfaction rate. With over 1,200 online orders each month and rising demand for franchises, Hayai continues to lead in fast, flavorful Japanese ramen across Indonesia.

Frequently Asked Questions

1 What types of ramen do you serve?

We offer a variety of ramen including classic tonkotsu, shoyu, miso, spicy miso, and vegetarian options. Each bowl is made with flavorful broth, fresh noodles, and quality toppings to suit every taste.

2 Do you offer takeout or delivery?

Yes! You can enjoy our ramen at home by ordering takeout directly from the store or through popular delivery apps. Fast, fresh, and convenient!

3 Are there any vegetarian or vegan options available?

Absolutely! We have delicious vegetarian ramen made with plant-based broth and toppings. Vegan options are also available—just let our staff know your preferences!
